# Phase 2 Tasks

Goal: Polish the top bar until it feels like the daily desktop baseline.

## Tasks

1. Tune bar spacing from real screenshots.
2. Tune glass opacity, border, highlight, and hover states.
3. Make workspace states clear: empty, occupied, active, urgent.
4. Make workspace rendering dynamic up to workspace 10.
5. Improve workspace app icon mapping.
6. Clamp active window titles so the left island never becomes too wide.
7. Make status labels compact on small screens.
8. Keep the bar readable on both bright and dark wallpapers.
9. Capture local screenshots for review, but do not commit screenshot images.

## Done When

- `qs -c march` opens a stable bar.
- The bar has no obvious text overflow.
- The bar looks acceptable on at least one bright wallpaper and one dark wallpaper.
- Workspaces can show up to 10 without breaking the left island.
- QML lint passes.
